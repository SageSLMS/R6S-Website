About Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released in 2015. The game puts heavy emphasis on environmental destruction and cooperation between players. Unlike other shooters, Siege aims for a more strategic experience where teamwork and tactics are crucial for victory.

Key features of the game include:

  • 5v5 multiplayer gameplay
  • Unique operators with special abilities
  • Destructible environments
  • High-stakes combat with no respawns
  • Regular updates and new content

Gameplay Mechanics

Rainbow Six Siege features unique gameplay mechanics that set it apart from other first-person shooters:

  • Preparation Phase: Attackers use drones to scout the area, while Defenders set up defenses.
  • Destruction: Most surfaces can be destroyed, allowing for creative tactics and dynamic gameplay.
  • Rappelling: Attackers can scale walls and breach from unexpected angles.
  • Leaning: Players can lean around corners to minimize exposure.
  • Reinforcements: Defenders can reinforce walls and hatches to prevent breaching.
  • Ping System: Allows for non-verbal communication and marking of enemy positions or gadgets.
  • One-Shot Headshot: Any weapon can kill with a single headshot, emphasizing precision.

Game Modes

Rainbow Six Siege offers various game modes to suit different playstyles and skill levels:

Casual

A relaxed mode for quick matches and practice. Features random objective locations and less stringent rules.

Unranked

Follows competitive rules but doesn't affect your rank. Good for practicing strategies in a more serious environment.

Ranked

The competitive mode where your performance affects your rank. Features longer matches and stricter rules.

Terrorist Hunt

A PvE mode where players face off against AI terrorists. Good for warming up or practicing aim.

Custom Games

Create private matches with custom settings. Ideal for team practice or experimenting with strategies.

Strategies

Success in Rainbow Six Siege requires a combination of skill, teamwork, and strategic thinking. Here are some key strategies to improve your gameplay:

Attacking Strategies

  • Drone effectively during the preparation phase to gather intel
  • Coordinate operator abilities for efficient breaching
  • Use vertical play to your advantage by destroying floors/ceilings
  • Implement crossfires when pushing objectives
  • Manage time effectively - don't rush, but don't wait too long

Defending Strategies

  • Set up a strong roam game to waste attackers' time
  • Use reinforcements and gadgets wisely to fortify the site
  • Create rotation holes for quick movement between rooms
  • Anchor objective rooms effectively with crossfires
  • Use information gathering tools (cameras, Valkyrie cams, etc.) to track attackers

General Tips

  • Communicate clearly with your team using callouts
  • Learn to use sound cues to your advantage
  • Practice your aim regularly in Terrorist Hunt or aim trainers
  • Stay updated on the latest meta changes and operator balancing
  • Learn from your mistakes by reviewing your gameplay

Esports

Rainbow Six Siege has a thriving competitive scene with major tournaments held worldwide.

Major Competitions

  • Six Invitational: The most prestigious annual R6 tournament, often considered the "World Championship"
  • Six Major: Held multiple times a year in different regions (Americas, Europe, Asia-Pacific)
  • Pro League: Regular season competition for professional teams, culminating in a LAN finals
  • Regional Leagues: Competitions specific to different regions, feeding into international events

Notable Teams

  • G2 Esports: European powerhouse, multiple-time world champions
  • Team Empire: Strong CIS region team, known for disciplined play
  • Spacestation Gaming: North American team, former Six Invitational champions
  • Ninjas in Pyjamas: Brazilian team with a strong international presence
  • DarkZero Esports: North American team known for strategic play

Viewing Esports

Rainbow Six Siege esports events are typically streamed on Twitch and YouTube. The official Rainbow Six Esports channels provide coverage of major events, with additional streams often available in multiple languages.

Updates and Future Content

Ubisoft regularly updates Rainbow Six Siege with new content and balance changes to keep the game fresh and competitive.

Seasonal Updates

Rainbow Six Siege operates on a seasonal model, with major updates typically released every three months. Each season usually includes:

  • New Operator(s)
  • Map reworks or new maps
  • Weapon and Operator balancing changes
  • New cosmetic items
  • Quality of life improvements

Year Pass and Battle Pass

Ubisoft offers yearly passes and seasonal battle passes that provide early access to new operators, exclusive cosmetics, and other benefits.

Future of Rainbow Six Siege
